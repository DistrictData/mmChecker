Frequently Asked Questions

What does this site do?

Intrare is a platform that connects NFT communities with a global online marketplace. We help provide NFT utility by providing holders access to discounts, perks, and other benefits of NFT ownership.

Is it safe to connect to MetaMask?

Yes. We only request READ access which you approve when you slick "Sign". You do not interact with smart contracts on our website. Allowing us to read your account provides us the ability to read metadata from the public blockchain and validate NFT ownership.
